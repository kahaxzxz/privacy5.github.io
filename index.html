Section 1: Our Data Handling Approach

  1. No Collection of Personal or Facial Data: Our apps are programmed to avoid the collection, storage, or dissemination of any user's personal or facial data.
  2. Local Data Processing: All app functionalities, particularly filters, operate locally on your device, ensuring no retention of images post-processing.
  3. Server-based Image Handling: In instances where image processing involves a server, the data is deleted immediately after usage.

Section 2: Types of Data Collected

  1. Non-personal Data Collection: While we avoid collecting Personal Information, we may gather Non-personal Information such as age, general location, device specifications, and app usage patterns.

Section 3: Data Received from External Sources

  1. Handling of Directly-submitted Information: Information received via our website or emails is processed selectively, primarily for app enhancement feedback.

Section 4: Data Retention and Use

  1. Data Utilization: In certain scenarios, necessary Personal Information from user communications may be used to resolve issues.
  2. Data Retention Period: User data is stored as needed for app functionality or as outlined above, and is either deleted or anonymized when its purpose is fulfilled.

Section 5: Information Sharing and Child Protection

  1. Child Privacy: In apps targeted at children, we ensure no personal data is shared.
  2. Adult Applications: For adult-focused apps, certain data may be shared with specific entities, not covered by this document.
  3. General Audience Applications: No Personal Information is collected, processed, or shared in our general audience apps.

Section 6: Special Provisions for Minors

  1. Inadvertent Data Collection: Immediate action is taken to delete any Personal Information collected from minors below the age limit.
  2. Parental Access: Parents can contact us at kahaxzxz@gmail.com for data modifications or deletions.

Section 7: Security and International Data Transfer

  1. Security Measures: We prioritize the security of your Personal Information, although absolute security cannot be guaranteed.
  2. Cross-border Data Transfer: Your information may be transferred internationally, and your submission of information signifies consent to these transfers.
